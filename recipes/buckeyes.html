Buckeyes

Description

This is a recipe for buckeyes, an Ohio delicacy made with peanut butter and chocolate. They look like buckeyes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups peanut butter
  • 6 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 pound butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vat of melting chocolate

Steps

  1. Combine butter, peanut butter, and vanilla extract in microwave safe bowl
  2. Microwave until butter is melted
  3. Gradually add powdered sugar while mixing with fork
  4. Roll into 1 inch balls
  5. Dip balls in chocolate
  6. Refrigerate for 1 hour
