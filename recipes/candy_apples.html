Candy Apples

Description

This is a recipe for red cherry candy apples. Some people prefer cinnamon, but you'll have to look elsewhere for that.

Ingredients

  • 50 Granny Smith apples
  • 10 pounds granulated sugar
  • 4 pounds corn syrup
  • 8 teaspoons red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon cherry flavoring

Steps

  1. Add corn syrup, sugar, and food coloring to kettle
  2. Cook over high heat until mixture reaches 285°F. While cooking skewer apples with wooden sticks
  3. Add cherry flavoring
  4. Continue cooking until mixture reaches 315°F
  5. Remove mixture from heat
  6. Dip apples, shaking off excess candy. Set on smooth surface
  7. Let cool for 1 hour
