Fudge

Description

This is a recipe for Calico Cottage chocolate fudge. Most of the fudge you'll find at amusement parks and in stores that don't produce it in house is from Calico Cottage. Successfully producing this fudge requires a Calico Cottage fudge mixer. I'll assume you have one.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups water
  • 1 box Calico Cottage chocolate fudge mix
  • 2 pounds butter
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Set mixer heat to 5 1 2
  2. Add butter, water, and both small bags of fudge mix
  3. After 5 minutes, turn off heat and add 4 large bags of fudge mix
  4. After 10 minutes set heat to 5 1 2 and add vanilla extract
  5. Let mix for 20 minutes
  6. Poor fudge into 6lb pans and let set over night
